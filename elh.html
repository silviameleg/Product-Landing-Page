every little heart

You are a champion

is a elh project that aims to bring the champion out of every little heart. We truly believe there is immense potential and brightness in each child and all they need is you and me believing in them and giving them a chance for a brighter future.
So join us and let's change these little hearts' destiny.

To find out more

You are a champion is a free programme and was created to help children and teenagers in financial difficulty. It has been proved that the best way to fight against poverty is to destroy the root of it. As great as it is to help someone in need, it's even greater to teach that person to provide for themselves and never again be in need. This is what the programme is all about. Our goal is to show these little hearts the great potential they have and to guide them towards a much better future. The practical side of things is simple: you can sign up for the programme here or perhaps you know someone that could benefit from this programme, you can propose them filling this form. To qualify, a candidate must be under de age of 18. The second step will be us getting in touch with you. And after the registration process is over, you are on for the most exciting experience! Our volunteer mentors will teach you how to create amazing games, mobile application and so much more! And the best part is, we have interactive learning classes for any age group, so every little heart is welcomed. Our partners will supply you with a free meal every day and we promise you will have fun! For the students that reach the age of hiring, there are employment opportunities with our partners and we will do our best to help you get the job of your dreams and destroy poverty in your life and your family’s for good. We look forward to meet you and change the world together!
